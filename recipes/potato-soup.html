Potato Soup

A delicious enactment of the classic potato soup

  • Avocado
  • Ground Beef
  • Cherry Tomatoes
  • Lime
  • Rice
  • Cilantro
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Chili Powder
  • Cumin
  • Onion Powder
  • Garlic Salt
  1. Cook ground beef, 1/2 diced onion, and garlic in a Dutch oven over medium heat until well browned. Stir in crushed spices.
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook Rice.
  3. Quarter Cherry Tomatoes, Halve Avocado, Dice Cilantro, Halve Lime. Combine, including remaining 1/2 onion
